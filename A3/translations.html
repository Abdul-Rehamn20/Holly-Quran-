Quran Translations

Available Translations

Currently, we offer the Urdu translation of the Holy Quran. We are working to add more translations in different languages in the future.

Urdu Translation

Our Urdu translation aims to convey the meaning of the Quran in clear, accessible language for Urdu speakers. The translation is carefully crafted to remain faithful to the original Arabic text while being easily understandable.

Sample Translation

Arabic:

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

Urdu:

اللہ کے نام سے جو بڑا مہربان نہایت رحم والا ہے
