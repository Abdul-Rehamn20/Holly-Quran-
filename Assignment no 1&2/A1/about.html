About Quran Majeed

Our Mission

Quran Majeed aims to make the Holy Quran accessible to everyone around the world. We strive to provide accurate translations, beautiful recitations, and educational resources to help people understand and connect with the divine message.

About the Holy Quran

The Holy Quran is the central religious text of Islam. Muslims believe it to be a revelation from God (Allah) and the final divine message to humanity, delivered through the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ over a period of 23 years, beginning in 610 CE.

The Quran consists of 114 chapters (surahs) which contain a total of 6,236 verses (ayat). The text is written in classical Arabic and has been preserved in its original form since its revelation over 1,400 years ago.
